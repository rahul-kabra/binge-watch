Binge Watch is your one stop shop for anything and everything related to movies, tv shows, celebs etc. and provides a very thorough and comprehensive detail about everything related to the former.

Tidbits

For the sake of convenience and also not to overwhelm the end user, the number of similar movies or shows in the detail specific pages has been restricted to 5. Also, this ensures that only the top matching results are returned.

For the movies list, shows list and search screens infinite scrolling has been implemented for an enhanced user experience. This does away with the problem of having to click on the different page numbers to naviagte to different pages.
